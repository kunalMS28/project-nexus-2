special's

Starter: Bruschetta
Bruschetta is a classic Italian starter that consists of grilled bread rubbed with garlic, topped with ripe tomatoes, fresh basil, and drizzled with olive oil. It's a flavorful and light appetizer.

Beverage: Coffee
Coffee is a globally cherished beverage, brewed from roasted coffee beans. Known for its rich flavor and stimulating effects due to caffeine, it has become a cultural icon, fostering social connections.

Main Course: Pad Thai
Pad Thai is a well-known Thai dish that features stir-fried rice noodles with a combination of ingredients like shrimp, tofu, bean sprouts, peanuts, and lime.

Dessert: Tiramisu
Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and a mascarpone cheese mixture, topped with cocoa powder. It's a delightful and indulgent sweet treat.

Snack: Sushi (Japanese)
Sushi, a Japanese delicacy, can also be enjoyed as a snack. Sushi rolls, such as maki rolls with seaweed, rice, and various fillings, make for a delicious and bite-sized snack option.
